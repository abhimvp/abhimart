# AbhiMart Return Policy

## General Return Policy

AbhiMart accepts returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in original packaging with all accessories included.

## Electronics Returns

Electronics including laptops, phones, tablets, headphones, and accessories can be returned within 30 days of delivery. The device must be factory reset before return. Original accessories, chargers, and packaging must be included. Items with physical damage, water damage, or signs of misuse are not eligible for return.

## Appliances Returns

Home appliances can be returned within 30 days if unused and in original packaging. Once an appliance has been installed or used, it cannot be returned unless it is defective. For defective appliances, contact support within 7 days of delivery.

## Fitness Equipment Returns

Fitness equipment can be returned within 30 days if unassembled and in original packaging. Assembled equipment cannot be returned unless defective. Large equipment like treadmills require a scheduled pickup — contact support to arrange.

## Books and Stationery Returns

Books can be returned within 30 days if unread and in original condition. Damaged or annotated books are not eligible. Stationery items must be unopened and unused.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Downloadable software or digital products
- Personal care items once opened
- Customized or personalized items

## How to Initiate a Return

Contact AbhiMart customer support with your order ID and reason for return. Our team will provide a return shipping label within 24 hours. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.
