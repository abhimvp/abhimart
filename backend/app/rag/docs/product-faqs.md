# AbhiMart Product FAQs

## Electronics FAQs

**Q: Are the electronics on AbhiMart genuine and original?**
A: Yes, all products on AbhiMart are 100% genuine and sourced directly from authorized distributors and brand partners.

**Q: Do laptops come with an operating system pre-installed?**
A: Most laptops come with Windows pre-installed. MacBooks come with macOS. Some laptops are sold without an OS — this is clearly mentioned in the product description.

**Q: Can I get a demo of a product before buying?**
A: We do not offer in-person demos. However, all products have detailed descriptions, specifications, and customer reviews. You can also chat with our support agent for guidance.

**Q: What if my phone or laptop has a defect out of the box?**
A: If you receive a defective device, contact support within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund — no questions asked.

## Appliances FAQs

**Q: Do you offer installation for appliances?**
A: Yes, free installation is available for select large appliances including washing machines, air conditioners, and dishwashers. Installation is scheduled after delivery.

**Q: What if the appliance does not fit in my home?**
A: Please check the product dimensions carefully before purchasing. If the item cannot be installed due to space constraints, it can be returned within 30 days if unused and uninstalled.

**Q: Do appliances come with a voltage stabilizer?**
A: No, stabilizers are not included unless explicitly stated in the product listing.

## Fitness Equipment FAQs

**Q: Do you assemble fitness equipment after delivery?**
A: Assembly service is available for large equipment like treadmills at an additional cost. Contact support to arrange. Smaller items like dumbbells and yoga mats do not require assembly.

**Q: Are the fitness products suitable for daily use?**
A: All fitness products on AbhiMart are rated for regular home use. Commercial-grade equipment for gyms may have different specifications — check the product description.

## Books and Stationery FAQs

**Q: Are the books new or used?**
A: All books sold on AbhiMart are brand new unless explicitly listed as "used" or "refurbished."

**Q: Can I return a book if I did not like it?**
A: Books can be returned within 30 days only if they are unread and in original condition. We cannot accept returns for books that have been read.

**Q: Do you sell international editions of books?**
A: Some books are available in international editions. This is clearly mentioned in the product listing. International editions have the same content but may differ in cover and print quality.

## General FAQs

**Q: How do I contact AbhiMart customer support?**
A: You can reach us via the chat support on our website, available 24/7. For complex issues, our human support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?**
A: Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. After that, the order goes into processing and cannot be cancelled. Once delivered, you can initiate a return.

**Q: Does AbhiMart price match?**
A: We do not currently offer price matching. However, we run regular sales and promotions. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
