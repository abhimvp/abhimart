# AbhiMart Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free for all orders above ₹999. Orders below ₹999 are charged a flat shipping fee of ₹99.

## Express Shipping

Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and is available for an additional ₹299. Express shipping is available for most pin codes across India.

## Same-Day Delivery

Same-day delivery is available in select metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune) for orders placed before 12 PM. Same-day delivery costs ₹199.

## Large Item Shipping

Large items such as treadmills, televisions, and large appliances have special shipping arrangements:

- Delivery takes 7-10 business days
- Includes free installation for applicable products
- A delivery team will contact you to schedule a delivery window
- Ensure someone is available at the delivery address

## Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order on the AbhiMart website or directly on the courier partner's website.

## Shipping Restrictions

Some products cannot be shipped to certain pin codes due to courier limitations. If your pin code is not serviceable, you will be notified at checkout.

## Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged, do not accept the delivery. Take a photo and contact support immediately. We will arrange a replacement at no cost within 48 hours.
