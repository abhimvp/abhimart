# AbhiMart Warranty Terms

## Electronics Warranty

All electronics sold on AbhiMart come with the manufacturer's warranty. Typical warranty periods are:

- Laptops and computers: 1 year manufacturer warranty
- Smartphones: 1 year manufacturer warranty
- Headphones and audio: 1 year manufacturer warranty
- Accessories and peripherals: 6 months manufacturer warranty

Manufacturer warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. It does not cover physical damage, water damage, or damage caused by misuse.

## Appliances Warranty

Home appliances come with manufacturer warranty ranging from 1 to 5 years depending on the product:

- Kitchen appliances (mixers, cookers): 1-2 years
- Large appliances (washing machines, refrigerators): 2-5 years
- Air purifiers and vacuums: 1-2 years

Appliance warranty includes free repair or replacement for manufacturing defects. In-home service is available for large appliances.

## Fitness Equipment Warranty

Fitness equipment warranty varies by product:

- Treadmills: 2 years on motor, 1 year on parts
- Dumbbells and weights: 1 year
- Wearables and fitness trackers: 1 year manufacturer warranty

## Books and Stationery Warranty

Books and stationery do not come with a warranty. If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact support within 48 hours for a free replacement.

## How to Claim Warranty

To claim warranty, contact AbhiMart support with your order ID and a description of the issue. For electronics and appliances, we will arrange a technician visit or direct you to the nearest authorized service center. Keep your invoice as proof of purchase — it is required for all warranty claims.

## Extended Warranty

AbhiMart offers extended warranty plans for electronics and large appliances. Extended warranty can be purchased at the time of buying the product and extends coverage by 1-2 additional years beyond the manufacturer warranty.
